US State Supplemental Privacy Notice to Residents of California, Colorado, Connecticut, Virginia, and Utah

Last updated: January 20, 2025

This Supplemental Privacy Notice applies to natural persons residing in the listed states. It is part of the QuickFundsWay privacy policy and describes how your personal information is collected, used, shared, and your associated rights.

Categories of Personal Information We Collect

We may collect identifiers, financial info, and government IDs from you or public sources. This info is used to deliver services, comply with law, and enhance user support. Cookies and usage data may also be collected.

Sharing and Sensitive Information

Sensitive personal data (e.g., SSN, financial account numbers) is only used as permitted by law. We do not use such data for profiling. Your data may be shared with third parties to fulfill services or comply with legal obligations.

Cookies and Tracking

Cookies are used to improve our service. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but it may impact site functionality.

Third-Party Links

External sites linked on our platform are not covered by our Privacy Policy. We advise reviewing the policies of all visited websites.

Retention Policy

Your data is retained as needed for service delivery and legal compliance, even after you stop using the service.

Minors

We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18. If this occurs, we delete such information upon identification.

Your Rights

  • Right to Know: What data we collect and how we use it.
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Right to Correct: Fix inaccuracies in your data.
  • Right to Opt Out: Stop use for targeted advertising.
  • Right Against Discrimination: No penalty for exercising rights.

To exercise your rights, contact support@onlineloannetwork.com or write to: Online Loan Network, 848 N. Rainbow Blvd #129, Las Vegas NV 89107.
